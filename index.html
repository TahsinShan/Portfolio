About Me

Hello! I’m Tahsin Hasan Shan, a curious mind who finds joy in building, exploring, and expressing. From writing code that brings ideas to life, to voicing thoughts through poetry, anchoring, and speeches — I believe in being both analytical and creative.

As the Founder and General Secretary of the Savar Cantonment Public School and College IT Club, I’ve led a growing community of tech-enthusiasts while staying hands-on with my own projects — including a sustainability-driven smart street system and a Mars rover prototype. I also recently won the regional round of the National High School Programming Competition, and I continue to challenge myself through small experiments and meaningful collaborations.

Many of my initiatives are inspired by real-world challenges — from pollution to energy use — with a focus on innovation that aligns with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Whether it's developing eco-friendly solutions, diving into Python, or exploring how technology can uplift communities, I’m always eager to learn, grow, and contribute.

At the heart of it all, I simply want to be the most raw and pure version of Shan — someone who builds, leads, and lives with purpose. As one of my favorite quotes goes:

“The best way to predict the future is to invent it.”
